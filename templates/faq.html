Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about how to get started with KUGuide, make the most of our resources, and troubleshoot common issues.

General

What is KUGuide?

KUGuide is a digital companion for Kuwait University students and graduates. It gathers study materials, discussion forums, and curated career guidance in one location.

Do I need an account to use the platform?

Yes. Sign in with your university credentials to unlock personalised features, bookmark resources, and track your progress.

Study & Community

Can I share notes with classmates?

Absolutely. Upload files such as PDF or DOCX directly to course communities to collaborate with your peers.

How do I join a course discussion?

After signing in, open your dashboard and select the course tile. From there, choose the discussion tab to start chatting.

Career guidance

Where can I find internships or job postings?

Visit the Career page to explore opportunities curated with the OSTA team. Filter listings by faculty or graduation year to find relevant matches.

How can mentors help me?

Mentors provide feedback on CVs, guide your interview preparation, and connect you to industry events. You can request a session from the career resources section.

Account & technical help

How do I reset my password?

Choose “Forgot password” on the sign-in page or visit the KU portal to complete the reset process.

Who do I contact for additional support?

Reach us from the Contact page. Include your university ID and a brief description so we can assist you quickly.
