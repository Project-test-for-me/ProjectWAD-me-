Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about how to get started with KUGuide, make the most of our resources, and troubleshoot common issues.

General

What is KUGuide?

KUGuide is a digital companion for Kuwait University students and graduates. It gathers study materials, discussion forums, and curated career guidance in one location.

Do I need an account to use the platform?

Yes. Sign in with your university credentials to unlock personalised features, bookmark resources, and track your progress.

Study Materials

Where can I find my course notes?

Course materials are organised by department. After logging in, select your major to view uploaded notes and recommended textbooks.

Can I contribute my own materials?

Yes! Students can upload study notes, summaries, or guides once approved by moderators to ensure quality and accuracy.

AI Assistant

How does the AI chatbot work?

The AI assistant helps answer academic questions, locate course resources, and provide university system guidance. It’s trained specifically for Kuwait University queries.

Can I talk to a real advisor?

Absolutely. The AI assistant complements, not replaces, human advisors. You can contact your faculty office or use the Contact page for personal assistance.

Career Support

How do I access OSTA opportunities?

Visit the Career section to explore current internships, training programs, and events in partnership with OSTA.

Do I need to pay for these services?

No. All KUGuide career and academic resources are free for current Kuwait University students and alumni.
